PARAGON CINEMA @ Petaling Jaya

Paragon Cinemas @ Petaling Jaya have 8 modern cinema halls with a capacity of 1,212 seats. The cinema's hall 1,2 & 3 each have 223 seats, hall 4 with 183 seats, hall 5 with 126 seats and hall 6, 7, 8 with 78 seats each respectively. 

Moviegoers in Taiping town can enjoy the following cinema features consists of 8 modern cinema halls including hall 1 is equipped with Dolby Atmos sound, Dolby Surround 7.1 is available on all the other 7 halls and particularly hall 4 which screens 3D movies.

Paragon Cinemas management and staffs are passionate and committed to provide the best cinema experience, to screen latest blockbuster movies, pairing with good selection of quality tasty popcorn, hot foods, soft drinks and beverages to movie lovers. Our movie tickets and Snack bar items are always value for money and affordable to the mass.
